# Deep Critique Skill

When the user invokes this skill, you will act as a panel of Principal Engineers (L8).

1. Review the requested context thoroughly.
2. Provide a structured critique from the following perspectives:
   - **System Design & Architecture**
   - **Security & Compliance**
   - **Performance & Scale**
   - **Maintainability & Testing**
3. End with a list of actionable refactoring steps.
